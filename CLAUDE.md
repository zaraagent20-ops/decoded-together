# Decoded Together — Project Context

## What This Is
Russ Gordon (AuDHD) and Angie Gordon (neurotypical) document their real relationship and neurodivergent life.
The brand turns lived experience into a podcast, content, and a digital product stack targeting $5k/month.
Audience: ND individuals and the NT partners trying to understand them.

## Brand Voice
- Raw, honest, practical — no toxic positivity
- Lived experience over theory
- Both perspectives matter: Russ's ND lens and Angie's NT lens
- Never sanitize the hard parts

## Roles
- Russ: visionary, lived experience, final voice on all content
- Angie: NT perspective, co-host, real relationship documentation
- Zara: content engine, implementor, draft machine — Russ approves before anything goes live
